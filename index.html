Tangled

Rapunzel is a beautiful princess with long and magical hair. She was kidnapped as a little baby by Mother Gothel, and since then she lived inside a tall tower all alone.

While Princess Rapunzel lived all alone in her tower, she was accompanied by her pet chameleon, Pascal.

Flynn Rider or Eugene Fitzherbert is a charming swashbuckler who arrived at Rapunzel's tower seeking for "safety."

Maximus is Flynn Rider's horse companion who went with Flynn Rider along his journey.
